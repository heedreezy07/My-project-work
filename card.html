Emerald Tree Boa

When born, the emerald tree boa is red and changes to emerald green as an adult.

  • Scientific Name: Corallus caninus
  • Average Length: 1.8 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Rainforests

Emerald tree boas live in the tropical rainforests of South America. They are a non poisonous species. They kill and eat their prey by suffecating them and swallowing them whole.
